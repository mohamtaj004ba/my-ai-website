Common questions

What's the $500 setup fee for?

It covers building and configuring your AI agent around your specific business — your services, call handling, lead routing, and integrations — plus porting or provisioning your number and testing everything before you go live.

Do I have to change my phone number?

No. We can forward your existing number to CallerCore, or provision a new one if you'd prefer. Either way your customers keep reaching you exactly like they do today.

What happens if I go over my included minutes?

Additional minutes are billed at $0.30/minute. Most businesses stay well within their plan — and you can move up a tier anytime if your call volume grows.

Can I try it before I commit?

Yes. Call our live demo line and hear the AI handle a real service call, or book a quick call and we'll walk you through it. There's also a 30-day money-back guarantee.
