The Problem

When calls go unanswered, the job usually goes somewhere else

Someone's on a job, with a customer, or it's after hours — so the phone rings out. The caller doesn't leave a voicemail. They call the next name on the list.

Calls slip through
Peak hours, after hours, weekends — the moments you're busiest are the moments calls get missed.
Callers don't wait
Most people who hit voicemail hang up and dial a competitor instead of leaving a message.
Revenue walks
Every missed call is a booked job that quietly went to someone else — and you never even knew.
No after-hours cover
Emergencies don't keep business hours. Without coverage, the 9pm burst pipe becomes tomorrow's lost customer.

CallerCore answers instantly, qualifies the lead, records the conversation, and triggers follow-up before the customer moves on — so your team stays focused on the work while the front office keeps moving.
